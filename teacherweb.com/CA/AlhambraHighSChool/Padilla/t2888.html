About The Teacher

NAME: Ms. Nancy Padilla

CLASS: Junior English, AP English Language, Freshman English

SCHOOL PHONE: 626-308-2342 


I have taught for several years and possess a strong background in AP 
English Language and Composition.  I have been one of the few AP teachers 
in the region that is invited by the College Board to join professors, 
writers of the exam, and other seasoned teachers to score the AP exams. I 
have taught all grades levels of English (grades 9-12) as well as middle 
school Language Arts. Additionally, I am certified in Social Studies and 
have taught World History, U.S. History, and AP U.S. History.  I have a 
passion for learning and continuously attend professional development 
trainings and college courses to keep updated on educational trends and 
strategies.  I love travelling and have spent many years visiting 
countries, mostly in northern Europe, not only vacationing but viewing 
places that I have taught and visit sites where the literary greats have 
lived and crafted their trade.

I am currently Chair of the English Department, Advisor to the AP 
Ambassador Club and Co-Chair of School Site Council.

My family is the most important thing in my life. I have one daughter and 
two dogs.
