April 16

THIS WAS TAKEN VERBATIM FROM AP BULLETIN SENT TO TEACHERS. 

AP Scores will no longer be mailed.  This year they will be Online. The 
scores come in July, but you should set up your account now to get ready 
(it's free).

1.  Sign up for an account at apscore.org.  Once you sign up, ou will 
receive emails about how and when to access your scores.  It's best to sign 
up for an account now to make getting scores in July as quick and easy as 
possible.

2.  Remember this information.
    Once you have taken our exams, just remember your College Board account
username and password, and one of the following:
    * Your AP number - you'll find it on the label in your AP Student Pack 
at exam time.
    OR
    * Your Student ID number if you put it on your AP answer sheet at exam 
time.

3.  Wait for an email - then receive your scores online.  You will receive 
and email after the exam period letting you know how and when to access your 
scores.
