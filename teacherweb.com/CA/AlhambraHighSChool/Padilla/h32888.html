CP Junior English Vocabulary

Lesson 1 - Word List

acolyte (n.) A person who assists in some religious service by carrying out 
minor duties; one who 
attends, a follower.

bibulous (adj.) - Give to the consumption of alcoholic drinks.  Highly 
absorbent.

coalesce (verb) To grow together; to unite to form a whole.

covert (adj) Not openly acknowlged; secret

declaim (verb) to recite something in the style of a public speech; to speak 
in a loud, theatrical way.

delineate (v) - to describe vividly and in detail.

demagogue (n) A leader who seeks power by appealing to people's emotions and 
prejudices and by 
making false claims and promises.

encomium (n) An expression of warm and enthusiastic praise; a tribute

obdurate (adj)  Resistant to persuasion; inflexible.  Persistent in 
wrongdoing.
